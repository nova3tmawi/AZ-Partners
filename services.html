What We Offer

At AZ Partners, we provide tailored consulting, training, and technical support to help organizations grow smarter, operate better, and deliver lasting impact. Every service we offer is built around your goals, your challenges, and your team.

Strategic Planning & Partnerships

We help you clarify your vision, define priorities, and build practical, results-driven strategies. Our team supports organizational assessments, strategic roadmaps, coalition building, and long-term planning tailored to your mission.

Project Management & MEAL

From concept to closure, we support full project cycle management — including planning, execution, monitoring, evaluation, and learning (MEAL). We specialize in building logical frameworks, KPIs, theories of change, and adaptive tools for donor-funded projects.

Fundraising & Resource Development

We work with you to craft compelling proposals, concept notes, and fundraising strategies. Whether you're approaching institutional donors or launching a grant campaign, we help position your ideas for success.

Training, Coaching & Capacity Building

We offer customized trainings and workshops for NGOs, SMEs, and teams across sectors. Topics include project design, proposal writing, leadership, financial management, communication, and more — all tailored to your context.

Advocacy, PR & Communication Strategy

Our team helps you develop powerful narratives, outreach strategies, and media campaigns. We support messaging design, visibility planning, digital content, and public engagement, especially for mission-driven work.

Events & Logistics Support

We manage planning, coordination, and implementation for conferences, launches, and training events. From strategic messaging to participant logistics, we ensure your events run smoothly and make an impact.

Research, Assessments & Baseline Studies

We conduct context analysis, risk assessments, and baseline studies to inform programming and funding decisions. Our reports are actionable, well-documented, and built around your strategic needs.

Custom Advisory Services

We also offer tailored support in areas like youth and women engagement, business development, internal restructuring, or digital strategy. Let’s design the solution that works best for you.
