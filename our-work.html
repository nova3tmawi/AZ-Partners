Real Solutions. Proven Impact.

Our portfolio reflects the diversity of our expertise and the depth of our commitment. We don't just consult — we design, implement, adapt, and empower.

Humanitarian Response & Emergency Coordination

We’ve led the planning, coordination, and implementation of rapid response projects — including operations in high-risk and conflict settings such as Gaza during wartime. Our contributions included coordinating food and shelter distribution, establishing IDP shelters (including one for over 10,000 orphans), mobilizing emergency funding, and drafting operational manuals.

Economic Empowerment & Green Solutions

Our interventions have empowered women, startups, and rural communities through home-based income-generating projects, business development support for green startups, and small-scale agriculture and renewable energy solutions.

Capacity Building & Training

AZ has delivered trainings and coaching in proposal writing, MEAL, strategy, leadership, communication, green entrepreneurship, and business planning, with follow-up mentorship to ensure applied skills development.

Operational Efficiency & Automation

We’ve helped NGOs and SMEs streamline their operations through workflow automation, digitization, and the development of project tracking tools, financial systems, and internal coordination platforms.

Recruitment & Talent Matching

We’ve supported partners in building effective, values-aligned teams by managing recruitment processes, designing job descriptions, conducting candidate screening, and supporting long-term HR strategies.
