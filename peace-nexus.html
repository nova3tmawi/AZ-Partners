About the Initiative

The Peace Nexus is a specialized initiative by AZ Partners dedicated to supporting cross-border collaborations that aim to build a sustainable peace. We provide targeted consulting, logistical support, and strategic guidance to NGOs, SMEs, and civil society organizations working on Israeli-Palestinian and regional cooperation projects.

Our goal is to strengthen the operational capacity of these vital organizations, helping them navigate complex challenges and maximize their impact. By fostering a network of exchange and shared learning, we aim to contribute to building the necessary infrastructure for a just and lasting peace.

Who We Support

  • Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) involved in cross-border peace initiatives.
  • Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) engaged in regional economic cooperation.
  • Joint ventures focused on shared social, environmental, or technological goals.
  • Donor-funded projects requiring expert local implementation and partnership facilitation.
